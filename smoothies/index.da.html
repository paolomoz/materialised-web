PRO TIPS

Perfect Smoothie Secrets

Layer It Right

Start with liquids at the bottom, then soft ingredients, and frozen items on top. This creates a vortex that pulls everything down into the blades.

Ice Ratio Matters

Use 1 cup of ice per 2 cups of other ingredients. Too much ice makes it thick; too little and it won't be cold enough.

Speed Control

Start on low and gradually increase to high. This ensures everything blends evenly without air pockets.
